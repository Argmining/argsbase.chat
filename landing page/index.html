ARGSBASE:
Multi-Agent Interface for Structured Human–AI Deliberation

✦ Where humans and AI become reasoning partners — not just answer providers.

ArgsBase is a deliberation interface that enables users to engage with multiple LLMs coordinated by a moderator agent — assigning roles, managing turn-taking, and ensuring structured interaction. Grounded in argumentation theory, it fosters epistemically responsible collaboration through user–LLM dialogues, real-time summaries, and argument maps.

Instead of treating LLMs as mere answer providers, ArgsBase positions them as reasoning partners — aligned with the vision of "reasonable parrots," where LLM agents interact guided by argumentative principles such as relevance, responsibility, and freedom. Our user study shows participants found the tool easy to use, perspective-enhancing, and promising for research.
