ArgsBase: Human-AI Deliberation

Welcome To ArgsBase

Where humans and AI think better, together.

ArgsBase is a platform for structured human–AI deliberation—where you can explore ideas through guided discussions with multiple AI models.

Here, you don't just ask questions—you engage in a conversation.

How it works?

  • DeepSeek acts as the moderator, keeping the discussion focused and structured
  • Command R and LLaMA participate as deliberators, offering different perspectives
  • You take part in the conversation, challenging ideas and guiding the direction

Stay organized as you think. A dedicated side panel helps you follow and refine the discussion:

  • A live summary of key points
  • Areas of agreement
  • Open questions
  • A map of the main arguments

Everything stays clear, structured, and easy to revisit.
